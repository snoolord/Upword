Upword
by
Winston Zhao

Instructions
1. Highlight a word by double clicking or dragging with the mouse
2. Right click the selection and look for "Look Upword" in the context menu
3. The Upword module presents words separated by part of speech and synonyms split by definition
4. Hovering over synonyms will change the word that was looked up in Gmail.
Upword can work on many different text fields but only Gmail is supported right now
